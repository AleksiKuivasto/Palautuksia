# RoomLight – One Design, Perfect Lighting in Every Room


Controlling the lighting of a hotel room can be confusing. Multiple switches control the same lights from different locations, leaving guests frustrated and the staff with extra tasks. 

RoomLight from Aleksin Ryhmä replaces this system with a simple but intuitive preset modes like “Atmosphere”, “Night” and “All lights on”. Hotels can design the perfect setup once, and sync it to every room after.

We believe technology in hotel rooms should remove complexity instead of adding to it. Lighting has a powerful impact on comfort and perception, yet it’s often overlooked or overcomplicated. With RoomLight we give hotels a way to create a premium, consistent lighting experience that works effortlessly for both guests and staff.

Getting started is simple. We work directly with hotel management to design the ideal lighting presets for one of their standard hotel rooms. Once approved, the configuration is synchronized to all rooms instantly and seamlessly. No complicated retraining. No confusing rewiring of guest behaviour. Just intuitive lighting, ready from day one.

“RoomLight has virtually eliminated lighting complaints while increasing positive guest feedback. It’s improved both the guest experience and our daily operations,” says a hotel manager.
